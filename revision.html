WHAT IS FORCE

Force is a push or pull on an object that causes it to move, stop, change shape or direction

Force is measured in Newtons (N) not grams (G) and is represented by the symbol F. It can be calculated using the formula F = m x a, where m is the mass of the object and a is its acceleration.
